Site Name

Connect&Learn Hub
A platform that bridges Grade 8–12 students with qualified tutors, promoting academic growth through accessible online learning. The “&” reflects the dual mission — connecting people and delivering knowledge. Suggested domain: connectlearnhub.co.za

Site Purpose

Connect&Learn Hub is an online tutoring platform for high school students in Grades 8–12. Learners and parents can discover subjects, understand available services, and enrol for sessions — making quality academic support easy to find and access.

  • View all tutoring subjects and their grade-level descriptions
  • Learn about tutor qualifications and the teaching approach
  • Submit an enrolment form to book tutoring sessions
  • Access contact details and frequently asked questions

Scenarios

Questions a Grade 8–12 student or parent might ask when visiting the site:

  • Which subjects are available for tutoring, and what grade levels are covered?
  • How do I enrol my child for sessions, and what is the cost per session?
  • What are the tutors’ qualifications, and do sessions run online or in-person?
  • How quickly can my child start, and what weekly time slots are available?

Typography

Poppins — Headings
Aa Bb Cc 123
Page headings h1–h3, navigation items, section titles. Bold (700–800) weight for impact and brand energy.
Roboto — Body Text
The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
Body copy, lists, form labels, descriptions. Light (300) to Regular (400) for comfortable reading.
